A [[Plane]] is a location on the [[Planar Dimension]] that has a distinct spiritual nature.

The known planes are: [(Ref)](https://discord.com/channels/1206338624334078062/1418137993197125713/1418336507135266910)
- The [[Material Plane]]
- The [[Fey Wild]]
- The [[Nine Hells]]
- The [[Abyss]]
- The elemental [[Plane of Water]]
- The elemental [[Plane of Earth]]
- The elemental [[Plane of Fire]]
- The elemental [[Plane of Air]]
- The [[Celestial Plane]]

All [[Plane]]s have both mortal [[Humanoid]]s and [[Spirit]]s inhabiting them, though in different proportions depending on the [[Plane]]. Contact between [[Plane]]s is possible with advanced [[Magic]], and travel between [[Plane]]s requires even more powerful [[Magic]].
